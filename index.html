About Us

Founded in 1973, DebtCollect Pro emerged from a simple vision: to help businesses recover their rightful earnings without compromising their professional relationships. What started as a small family-owned collection agency has grown into one of the nation's leading commercial debt recovery firms, with over $500 million recovered for our clients.

Our mission is to provide ethical, effective debt collection services that protect our clients' bottom line and reputation. We believe in transparency, professionalism, and results-driven solutions. Our core values of integrity, persistence, and excellence guide every interaction, ensuring we deliver the highest level of service to our clients while maintaining the dignity of all parties involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does your no-fee guarantee work?

Our contingency-based model means we only earn a fee when we successfully collect your debt. If we don't recover your money, you don't pay a penny. The fee is a percentage of the amount collected, agreed upon before we begin work.

What types of commercial debt do you collect?

We specialize in all forms of B2B debt, including unpaid invoices, service fees, commercial loans, lease payments, and contract disputes. We work across all industries and handle both domestic and international collections.

How long does the collection process typically take?

While each case is unique, we typically see results within 30-90 days. Our aggressive yet professional approach means we work quickly to recover your funds, with most successful collections happening in the first 45 days.

What makes your approach different from other agencies?

We combine sophisticated skip-tracing technology, legal expertise, and professional negotiation skills with a focus on preserving business relationships. Our team understands the nuances of B2B collections and maintains the highest professional standards.

How do you protect our business reputation during collections?

We understand the importance of maintaining professional relationships. Our approach is firm but diplomatic, always representing your business with the utmost professionalism. We follow all regulatory requirements and industry best practices.
